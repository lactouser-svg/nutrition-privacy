1. Information We Collect

We collect food scan images, barcode data, and user health preferences to provide nutrition analysis. Account information (email, name) is collected via Firebase Authentication. Scan history is stored in Firebase Firestore.

2. How We Use Your Information

To provide food analysis, track nutrition history, and process in-app purchases via Google Play Billing.

3. Camera and Photo Access

Camera and photo library access is used solely for food scanning. Images are processed via Google Cloud Vision API and stored securely.

4. Third-Party Services

We use: Google Firebase (authentication, database, analytics), Google Cloud Functions (data processing), Google Play Billing (subscriptions). These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard security measures.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data via the in-app "Delete Account" feature or by emailing us.

7. Children's Privacy

Vireo is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
