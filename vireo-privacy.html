Privacy Policy for Vireo

Last updated: January 2026

1. Data We Collect

Vireo collects food scan images, barcode data, and user preferences to provide nutrition analysis. We use Firebase Authentication for login and Firebase Firestore to store your scan history.

2. Camera & Photos

The app accesses your camera and photo library solely for food scanning purposes. Images are processed via Google Cloud Vision API and stored securely.

3. Third Party Services

We use Firebase (Google), RevenueCat (subscriptions), and Google Cloud Functions. These services may collect device identifiers and usage data under their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data via the in-app "Delete Account" feature or by contacting support.
