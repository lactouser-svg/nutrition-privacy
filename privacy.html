Eight AI Privacy Policy

Effective date:5 Oct 2025

1. Information we collect

On first launch we ask for:

  • Age
  • Height
  • Weight
  • Training

These data are used solely to calculate daily calorie and macro-nutrient estimates inside the app. We do NOT send this information to any server; everything stays on your device.

2. Third-party services

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics (screen opens, button taps). This service may collect your IP address, device identifiers and coarse location. Firebase data are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies & analytics

Our privacy page itself does not use cookies. The app does not use web cookies.

4. Data retention & deletion

Body-metric data you enter are stored locally and removed when you uninstall the app. Firebase Analytics data are kept by Google for 60 days.

5. Your rights

You may clear app data at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Eight AI → Storage → Clear Data.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, e-mail us at warming144000@gmail.com.

This policy is hosted with GitHub Pages and can change in the future. Continued use of the app after any change means you accept the revised policy.
